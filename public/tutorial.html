How to Build Your Chatbot

Follow this step-by-step guide to create your first AI chatbot in under 5 minutes!

1

Choose Your Template

Start by selecting a pre-built template that matches your use case. We have templates for customer service, lead generation, appointment booking, e-commerce, and more. Each template comes with pre-configured responses and flows to get you started quickly.

Pro Tip:

If you're new to chatbots, start with the "Customer Service Pro" template - it's the most versatile and beginner-friendly!

2

Customize Your Bot's Personality

Give your chatbot a name and personality. Choose a friendly name like "Sarah" or "Alex", and craft a welcoming message that reflects your brand's tone. This is what users will see first when they interact with your bot.

Pro Tip:

Keep your welcome message short and action-oriented. Tell users exactly what your bot can help them with!

3

Set Up Responses

Configure how your bot responds to common questions. You can add multiple response options for variety, include quick reply buttons for common actions, and set up fallback messages for when the bot doesn't understand a question.

Pro Tip:

Add emojis to your responses to make them more engaging and friendly. Users love chatbots with personality!

4

Test Your Chatbot

Use the live preview to test your chatbot before deploying it. Try different questions and scenarios to make sure it responds correctly. The preview updates in real-time as you make changes.

Pro Tip:

Test your bot with questions real customers might ask. Think like your users!

5

Deploy & Share

Once you're happy with your chatbot, you can deploy it to your website with a simple embed code, download it to your phone for testing, or share it with friends and colleagues for feedback.

Pro Tip:

Start with a soft launch - share your bot with a small group first to gather feedback before going live!
